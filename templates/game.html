How the Game Works:

  • After 2 players enter the queue they are placed in a room
  • Player 1 sets a number between 1 and 10.
  • Player 2 tries to guess it.
  • If Player 2 guesses correctly within 5 rounds, they win. Otherwise, Player 1 wins.
  • Each round allows one guess from Player 2.
